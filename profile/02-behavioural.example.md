# Behavioural Examples (STAR Format)

> These examples are used to prepare for behavioural interview questions and to add
> authentic detail to cover letters. Each one uses the STAR framework:
> **Situation → Task → Action → Result**.

---

## 1. Leadership

**Situation:** [Describe the context — team, company, challenge.]

**Task:** [What were you responsible for achieving?]

**Action:** [What did you specifically do? Be concrete.]

**Result:** [What was the measurable outcome?]

---

## 2. Conflict Resolution

**Situation:** [Describe the context — who was involved, what the tension was.]

**Task:** [What did you need to resolve or navigate?]

**Action:** [How did you handle it?]

**Result:** [How was it resolved? What did you learn?]

---

## 3. Dealing with Ambiguity

**Situation:** [Describe the ambiguous or undefined situation.]

**Task:** [What were you asked to do, even without clear direction?]

**Action:** [How did you clarify, explore, and proceed?]

**Result:** [What did you deliver or uncover?]

---

## 4. Delivering Under Pressure

**Situation:** [Describe the constraint — deadline, scope, limited resources.]

**Task:** [What did you need to deliver and by when?]

**Action:** [How did you prioritise and execute?]

**Result:** [What was the outcome? Include metrics if possible.]

---

## 5. Cross-Functional Collaboration

**Situation:** [Describe the team and stakeholders involved.]

**Task:** [What shared goal were you working toward?]

**Action:** [How did you coordinate across teams or disciplines?]

**Result:** [What did you achieve together?]

---

## 6. Failure and Learning

**Situation:** [Describe what went wrong or what feedback you received.]

**Task:** [What did you need to address or change?]

**Action:** [What steps did you take to improve?]

**Result:** [What changed? What do you do differently now?]

**Lesson learned:** [One clear takeaway.]
