# Interview Preparation

---

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer

- What would a typical day for me in this role look like?
- What does success look like in this position?
- What is the biggest problem the team is facing that you are hoping the person in this role will solve?
- What's the performance review process like here? How often would I be formally reviewed?
- What's your favourite part of working here?
- What are the current goals that the company is focused on, and how does this team work to support hitting those goals?

## Company Research Checklist

- [ ] Company mission and values (from their website, not just the job ad)
- [ ] Recent news, projects, or product launches
- [ ] Who you're interviewing with (LinkedIn, company bio page)
- [ ] Company size, structure, and industry position
- [ ] Glassdoor / Seek reviews (note patterns, take with a grain of salt)
- [ ] Their tech stack or design system (if relevant to the role)
- [ ] Any public-facing work you can reference (apps, websites, reports)
