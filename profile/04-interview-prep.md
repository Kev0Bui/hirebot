## Questions to Ask the Interviewer

<!--
Prepare 5–8 questions. Good questions show you've researched the company
and are evaluating fit — not just hoping to be picked. Avoid questions
easily answered by reading the job ad or company website.
-->

- "What does success look like in this role after 6 months?"
- "How is the team structured, and who would I collaborate with most closely?"
- "What's the biggest challenge the team is facing right now?"
- "How does the organisation approach [relevant topic — e.g. accessibility, content governance, agile delivery]?"
- "What's the team's approach to professional development?"

## Company Research Checklist

<!--
Before every interview, research the company. Use this checklist to make sure
you've covered the basics. Claude can help you with this — just ask.
-->

- [ ] Company mission and values (from their website, not just the job ad)
- [ ] Recent news, projects, or product launches
- [ ] Who you're interviewing with (LinkedIn, company bio page)
- [ ] Company size, structure, and industry position
- [ ] Glassdoor / Seek reviews (take with a grain of salt, but note patterns)
- [ ] Their tech stack or design system (if relevant to the role)
- [ ] Any public-facing work you can reference (apps, websites, reports)
